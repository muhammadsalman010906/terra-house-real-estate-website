Our Story

Two Decades of Building Trust, Brick by Brick

Founded in 2006 by Farooq Ahmed, Terra House began as a modest construction outfit with a singular promise: deliver every project with honesty, craftsmanship, and an eye for timeless design. Today, that promise has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in real estate marketing and premium construction.

From plot files in the Capital's most coveted societies to full-scale interior fit-outs for embassies and multinational offices, Terra House has quietly become the contractor of choice for clients who refuse to compromise on quality. Our in-house teams of architects, master carpenters, plumbers, and site engineers work in lock-step — meaning fewer vendors, faster timelines, and a single point of accountability: us.

Under Farooq Ahmed's leadership, Terra House has built a reputation for dominating the market not through volume, but through relationships — repeat clients, referred families, and corporate partners who return project after project.
